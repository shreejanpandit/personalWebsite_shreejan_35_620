Who am I ?






I am Shreejan, I am born and raised in Pokhara-Nepal, and currently I am pursuing B.E in software engineering, from Gandaki College Of Engineering and science.

I have done my schoolings from Brahma Rupa Secondary School with grade A in SLC and 71% marks in 12th .

Talking about my family, my family consists of six members including me and my elder sisters. My father is teacher in Brahma Rupa Secondary School and mother is a housewife.

My hobbies include watching movies, spending time to my friends and playing indoor games.

Coming to my strengths and weaknesses, my strengths are. I am a good learner, innovative, I have positive attitude and committed to my work. My weakness is lacking confidence and difficulty asking questions.

My ultimate goal is to become responsible & knowledgeable personality & on respected position

That's all about myself.
